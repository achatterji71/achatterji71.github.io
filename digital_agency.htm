The Digital Agency Project

Sustaining the energy for lasting change — in the hands of those who will carry it forward.

Family · Places · Interests
The Problem

How might we sustain the energy that results in lasting positive impact from the desire for such change from a cohort of well-meaning, resourceful individuals and organisations?

The world is not short of people who want to make it better. It is short of the infrastructure that turns that desire into something durable. Good intentions, adequate resources and genuine commitment are necessary — but they are not sufficient. The gap between wanting change and achieving it is where most efforts quietly expire.

The Digital Agency Project is an early-stage initiative built around a single conviction: that the next generation of changemakers — in the US Mid-Atlantic, in South and Southeast Asia, and in middle-income societies more broadly — can achieve what previous generations missed if they are given the right tools, the right relationships, and the right framework for using their privilege to generate agency in others rather than dependency.

Technology is not the solution to this problem. But in a moment when technology is being projected as overwhelming humanity, it is a necessary part of the answer — used with intention, equity and humility.

What We Do
Mentorship
Working directly with young professionals to build the confidence, frameworks and relationships they need to lead change on their own terms.
Tools for Digital Equity
Building and curating digital tools that expand access — not just to technology, but to the agency that technology can enable.
Writing & Publishing
Making the frameworks and lessons of three decades of professional experience available — clearly, accessibly, and without the gatekeeping of traditional publishing.
Training & Workshops
Structured learning experiences for teams and cohorts working on complex social challenges — designed to build capacity that outlasts the workshop.
Consulting & Advisory
Working with impact investors and changemakers to sharpen strategy, stress-test assumptions and identify the conditions for durable impact.
Geography

Middle-income societies broadly — with initial focus on the US Mid-Atlantic, South Asia and Southeast Asia, where proximity and familiarity create the conditions for relationships rather than transactions.

Where We Are

The Digital Agency Project is at an early concept stage. The problem is well-defined. The team is small and the work is beginning. What we are building toward — the frameworks, the tools, the relationships, the body of writing — will take shape in the coming years. We are not in a hurry. Lasting change rarely is.

Get Involved

If you are an impact investor, a changemaker, or someone who recognises this problem from your own experience — we would like to hear from you. The Digital Agency Project is at the stage where the right conversations matter more than the right deck.
