Workplaces

A professional life in six chapters — idealism, exploitation, scale, agency, reckoning, and giving away.

Family · Places · Interests

Every professional life has a shape, even if you can only see it in retrospect. Mine looks something like this: begin with genuine idealism, get ground down by systems designed to extract rather than develop, find unexpected scale, attempt something of your own, reckon with the ways you became what you once resisted, and then — with whatever remains — try to give it away usefully.

These are the workplaces where that arc played out. The stories are coming in Phase 2.
