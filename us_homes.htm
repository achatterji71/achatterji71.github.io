Our Homes

Six homes since 1996 — and a few more chapters still being written.

Family · Places · Interests

As of 2005, we had lived in six homes in the United States — four apartments and two that we owned (theoretically, since the deeds were held by our generous bank, who kindly extended us the opportunity to live in neck-deep debt, happily ever after). Each of these homes has special memories.

The apartments tell the story of an itinerant engineer and his family finding their footing in a new country. The houses that followed tell a different story — of putting down roots, of children growing up, of a family defining itself. And then came Manila, which reminded us that home is not always where you expect to find it.
