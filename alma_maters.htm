The institutions that shaped a mind — in Meerut, Rourkela, and Washington DC.

Family · Places · Interests

Three institutions, spread across four decades and two countries. Each one transformative in a different way — the first building character, the second building friendships and technical foundations, the third completing a circle that had been left open for eight years.

The stories from all three are told in their own sections.
