Around the World

A life measured, in part, in passports and boarding passes.

Family · Places · Interests

When this site was first written in 2005, I had visited thirteen countries — mostly India, the United States, and a handful of others. It felt like a reasonable start. Meenakshi and I both inherited a passion for travel from our respective parents, and we had been blessed with opportunities to visit parts of Asia, Europe, and North America.

What happened next was not entirely planned. The years between 2005 and 2026 took me to places I had only read about — across six continents, through deserts and archipelagos, ancient cities and wild coastlines. The number grew slowly at first, then faster, then became something of a quiet mission.

The stories from these travels — the places, the people, the moments that stay with you long after the jet lag fades — are coming in Phase 2 of this site. There is a great deal to write.

With some luck, the count will be at 88 by April 2026.
