Rourkela · 1990–1992

Hall 2

The final two years — a private room, and the luxury of solitude.

Family · Places · Interests

I stayed in Hall 2 during the last two years — the best dorm of all in our college, one of the two at the time that had private rooms. There is something to be said for a room of one's own after two years of four to a room. Hall 2 sat atop a hill, and the view from it has stayed with me long after the engineering has faded.
